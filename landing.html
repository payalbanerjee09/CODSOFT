How AbhiYu & Co. Started

AbhiYu & Co. started with a simple thought between two souls, Abhii and Payu: "The perfect conversation always begins with a perfect cup of coffee." What began as a shared love for late-night brews and sweet cravings soon blossomed into a premium aesthetic space.

Every blend we serve is hand-selected, and every cake is baked from scratch with love. For us, this isn't just a coffee shop—it's a warm corner where beautiful memories are created, one sip and one slice at a time.
